Stacked Ensemble Classifier for Adoption of Point-of-Collection Water Treatment Technology Among Households in Western Kenya

Robert Omondi Oluoch, Herbert Imboga, Anthony Waititu, Susan Mwelu, Illah Evance, Ferdnand Ongeta • International Journal of Data Science and Analysis • 2025

Developed and implemented a stacking ensemble classifier to model adoption of chlorine dispensers among 27,457 households in western Kenya. Addressed challenges of monitoring technology adoption during COVID-19 pandemic using machine learning on imbalanced datasets.

Assessing factors affecting the use of chlorine dispensers for household water treatment in rural Kenya, Malawi, and Uganda

Illah Evance, Ferdnand Ongeta, Sharon Chebet, Andrew Kitchel, Maarten van de Reep, Moses K. Baraza, James Maina, Andrew Onyango • Journal of Water, Sanitation and Hygiene for Development • 2025

Assessed factors influencing community adoption of chlorine for water treatment across three African countries. Study used repeated cross-sectional data from 2021-2022 to identify barriers affecting achievement of Sustainable Development Goal 6.
